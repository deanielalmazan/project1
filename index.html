Breathtaking Power. Featherweight Design.

Introducing the new iPhone Air — thinner, lighter, and faster than ever.

The iPhone Air lives up to its name. At just 5.6 mm, it's the thinnest iPhone ever made, and at 165 grams, it's among the lightest too.

Apple uses a titanium frame for strength, paired with Ceramic Shield 2 on both the front and back for durability. The result is a phone that feels feather-light in your hand but still tough enough for daily life in Nairobi.

This design is classic “Air”: slim, minimal, premium.

Power That Matches the Pros

Don't let the size fool you — the iPhone Air runs on the same A19 Pro chip found in the iPhone 17 Pro models. Whether you're gaming, multitasking, or using Apple's latest AI features, the Air keeps up effortlessly.

Its 6.5-inch Super Retina XDR OLED display supports ProMotion 120 Hz refresh rates and peaks at 3,000 nits brightness. From office use to outdoor adventures, the iPhone Air stays bright and smooth.

Simplified Yet Smart Cameras

The Air doesn't carry the triple-lens setup of the Pro series, but it doesn't need to. Instead, it comes with:

  • 48 MP Fusion main camera with advanced stabilization.
  • 2x optical-quality zoom achieved through sensor cropping.
  • 18 MP Center Stage front camera, perfect for video calls and selfies.

It's designed for everyday creators, social-media lovers, and casual photographers who want quality without complexity.

Battery & The Exclusive Air Battery Pack

Being the thinnest iPhone has one trade-off: there isn't space for a massive battery. Apple promises all-day battery life, but to go further, they've introduced a new magnetic Air Battery Pack accessory.

This lightweight snap-on pack adds extra hours of use without compromising the Air’s slim profile. It's a clever move that makes the Air more flexible for busy days or long travels.

Storage & Colors
